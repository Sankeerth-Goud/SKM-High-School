About SKM High School

Honoring Legacy, Building Future

Our Inspiring Journey

Established in 2020 in Muneerabad, SKM High School honors the legacy of late Shri Khannamma. We are committed to the holistic development of every student, nurturing their academic excellence while building strong character, patriotic values, and a deep sense of social responsibility. Our institution stands as a beacon of quality education in the community.
